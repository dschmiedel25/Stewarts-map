How Bathroom Report works

What this is

Bathroom Report helps you find a clean, open public bathroom fast — at convenience stores, travel stops, and other reliable spots. It’s community-powered and growing nationwide.

Ratings

Ratings come from people who’ve actually been there — you can only rate when you’re on-site. They cover the bathroom itself, so you know what to expect before you go.

Hours & open status

We show hours when we have them. A spot with unknown hours still appears — we just can’t confirm it’s open, so we never mark it closed on a guess. Hours can occasionally be outdated; call ahead if it’s critical.

What you can do

Get directions in your preferred maps app, share a spot, or report wrong info — all from a pin. Sign in (optional) to rate, add tips, build your Passport, and sync across devices.

Missing a spot?

Send it in from the map. We add locations by hand to keep the data accurate.

How locations get added

Every location is reviewed and added by hand, so the map stays accurate instead of auto-importing bad data.

Accounts & privacy

Signing in is optional — the whole map works without an account. Sign in to rate, add tips, and build your Passport, synced across your devices. Like most sites, Bathroom Report also uses basic analytics to understand overall usage; it doesn’t identify you personally.

Not affiliated

Bathroom Report is an independent, community-created project. It isn’t affiliated with or endorsed by any business listed.

Keep it ad-free

Bathroom Report is independent — no ads, no investors — just me keeping the map accurate and the servers on. Chipping in helps keep it running and free.

FAQ

Do I need an account? No — the map, directions, and Bathroom Now all work signed out. An account only adds rating, tips, and Passport.

Why can’t I rate this bathroom? You need to be on-site (within about a third of a mile), so ratings come from real visits.

The hours are wrong, or it’s closed. Report it from the pin and we’ll review it.

I don’t see a store I know is there. We add locations by hand — send it in and we’ll add it.

Is this affiliated with these chains? No. It’s an independent, community-created project.
