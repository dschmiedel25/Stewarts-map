🚽 Welcome!

Quick tour:

  • 📍 Tap any pin to rate the store & bathroom, leave a tip, or get directions.
  • ✅ Just want to log a visit without rating anything? Tap "I've been here" instead.
  • 🕐 Hours shown are live — the map knows if a spot is open right now.
  • 🧭 First time using Directions, you'll pick Google Maps, Waze, or Apple Maps — it's remembered after that.
  • 🔗 Share a pin, or 🚩 Report a problem (wrong info, permanently closed, etc.) — right in each pin's popup.
  • 📋 Use List to browse every location, or Bathroom Now to find the closest open bathroom by driving distance.
  • 👤 Log in (just a username & password, no email) to make your name and ratings follow you across devices, and to automatically appear on the Leaderboard under your username. No password recovery exists — if you forget it, that username is gone for good.
